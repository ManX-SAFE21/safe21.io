The problem

Self-custody is freedom — and responsibility.

Holding your own keys means no bank can freeze or lose your Bitcoin. It also means two risks sit squarely on you. SAFE21 closes both.

Lose the keys, lose the coins

A misplaced seed phrase, a broken device, a forgotten backup — and your Bitcoin is gone for good. We help you set up storage that survives real life.

If something happens to you

Your family may never recover coins they can't access. We help you plan a clear, decentralised inheritance — no notary, no custodian, no single point of failure.
